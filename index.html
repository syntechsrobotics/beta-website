ABOUT US VEX

The end of Tower Takeover

We made a valiant effort in BC Provincials and we were so close to qualifying to Worlds, but to no avail. Thank you everyone for supporting us along the way and we will see you next season!

Also, big congrats to our middle school teams for getting Tournament Finalists (6408S), Amaze (6408S), and Build (6408Z) Awards!

Syntechs Robotics is a student-led robotics club at Heritage Woods Secondary, striving with confidence towards the VEX Tower Takeover Provincials.
