Our Sponsors

A look at how our sponsors contribute to the club

Mircrosoft was how we got into FRC with the help of the school. FRC is a very costly sport and Microsoft helped us become the first school in SD43 to compete in robotics tournaments. Their leadership helped bring robotics closer to students than ever before, and we thank them profusely for helping kickstart our club.

Microsoft Canada

Our parent committee and the school have played a major role in the success of Syntechs Robotics. The council has put in a lot of effort into making Syntechs a successful club with purchasing equipment, parts, and this website domain. We're also very grateful to the school for letting us stay into the night to finish our robots!

Heritage Woods Parent Advisory Committee

NTN's contribution in parts (specifically screws and nuts) has been essential to us building our robots because if we had no screws or nuts, how would we be able to compete!? It's an essential part of building a robot, and having NTN as a sponsor has lifted the burden of not having to worry about our supply.

NTN Bearings

Windsor Plywood has made our lives a little easier when transporting and storing our robots. They sent over a ton of plywood for our eager woodworking students to make boxes for the robots that fit to the specification of the competitions. Recently, they sent over some more plywood and some hinges so we could make lids as well!

Windsor Plywood - Coquitlam

Unbounce and Manterra recently made it more accessible than ever for students to join thanks to their amazing contributions which allowed us to lower the cost for students to join as well as order new parts. This helped us form six whole teams with parts and tools for each and everyone of them.

Unbounce
